Dijon Cream Pork

Description

This time of year, recipes for easy-to-prepare weeknight meals are more important to me than ever. I affectionately refer to them as "flash-in-the-pan meals", because, in a flash, in one pan, with no stress, I am eating something delicious -- in the case of this recipe, it is elegant enough to serve to company as well.

Ingredients

  • Pork Medallions
  • Olive Oil
  • White Wine
  • Chicken Stock
  • Dijon Mustard
  • Heavy Cream
  • Butter
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Brown Pork medallions, seasoned with SnP, in olive oil
  2. Remove pork medallions from heat
  3. Deglaze the pan with white wine
  4. Add some Chicken Stock
  5. Add 2 tbsp Dijon Mustard
  6. Add pork medallions back in and heavy cream
  7. Add a knob of butter
  8. SnP to taste, bring to desired consistency
